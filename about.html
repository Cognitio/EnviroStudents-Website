About Us
We’re a group of motivated students set on changing our community for the better.

The Environmental Community of Hamilton Students (ECHS) is a non-profit aimed at connecting environmentally-conscious students throughout the city and cultivate environmentalism through the sharing of ideas and joint student action. As students involved in the community, we noticed a lack of youth spaces in the fight for climate change, as our voices were frequently ignored as 'unimportant' and 'inexperienced'. Students may face difficulty accessing resources, directly leading to less youth-directed action, and an ultimately lowered public awareness. ECHS was created as a resource to serve as collective youth voice offering student groups more influence and credibility at higher institutional levels. Though student-oriented, ECHS also includes graduates and individuals without the credibility of a large environmental organizations, all the while working closely with established organizations towards improving . ECHS also allows for community involvement from larger environmental groups unaffiliated with a particular school, creating opportunity for inter-school projects  leading to a larger, longer-lasting impact on the community.  With more to come soon, we hope that we will be able to change the mindset and environment of Hamilton - and the world - for the better.

Our Goals

Sustainability
ECHS aims to promote more sustainable practices in our community such as buying local, recycling, and efficient transport.
Combating Climate Change
As a major issue face humans in the 21st century, ECHS aims to help in the battle against climate change.
Improving our Environment
Through initiatives such as park cleanups, ECHS members contribute to help improve our local environment.
Education
We aim to provide educational services to the community to promote environmental awareness.
Evaluating Long Term Outcomes
Through our work we hope to make meaningful change that benefits everyone in the long-term. Our efforts to change government policy are part of this goal.
Community Engagement
We aim to engage with and help the community in environmentally sustainable practices and to promote conscious lifestyle through our work.
